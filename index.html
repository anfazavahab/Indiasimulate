README.md

IndiaSimulate · Social Protection Atlas

This repository is the visualisation and data layer of IndiaSimulate — a microsimulation model for India's social protection programmes, inspired by SOUTHMOD and EUROMOD. The Atlas provides state-level comparative data across three policy domains as the empirical foundation for distributional analysis.

Relation to IndiaSimulate

IndiaSimulate is being developed as India's first open-access social protection microsimulation model, starting with Kerala (v0.1). This Atlas repository provides:

  • The comparative benchmarks against which Kerala-specific simulations are interpreted
  • A public-facing data layer that establishes the project's empirical credibility
  • A foundation for future state-level policy comparison modules
IndiaSimulate is inspired by SOUTHMOD (South Africa, Tanzania) and EUROMOD (EU-27). India — with 1.4 billion people and ₹10 lakh crore in annual social transfers — has no equivalent public microsimulation model. This project is a first step.
