IndiaSimulate · Social Protection Atlas

State-level visualisation atlas for India's social protection system, part of IndiaSimulate — a microsimulation model inspired by SOUTHMOD and EUROMOD.

Data sources

  • Pensions: State government orders; NSAP guidelines, Ministry of Rural Development
  • Health insurance: Vahab & Drèze (2025), The India Forum; state budget documents
  • MGNREGS: Ministry of Rural Development gazette notification, March 2024

Relation to IndiaSimulate

IndiaSimulate is India's first open-access social protection microsimulation model (v0.1, Kerala scope). This Atlas provides the comparative state-level empirical foundation for distributional analysis.

India — 1.4 billion people, ₹10 lakh crore in annual social transfers — has no public microsimulation model equivalent. This project is a first step.
